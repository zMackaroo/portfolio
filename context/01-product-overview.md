# Product Overview

## Project Name

**Personal Developer Portfolio Website**

## Purpose

A visually striking personal portfolio website for a UI/UX designer and software engineer. The site is designed to communicate professional identity, showcase past work experience and featured projects, highlight technical skills, and invite collaboration or job opportunities — all through a premium dark-themed aesthetic.

## Target Audience

- Hiring managers and recruiters at tech companies
- Cross-functional product teams looking for design-engineering talent
- Potential freelance clients with digital product needs

## Core Value Proposition

The portfolio presents the owner as someone who sits at the intersection of design and engineering — a rare profile that "judges a book by its cover," meaning they care deeply about both visual quality and technical execution. The design itself is proof of that claim.

---

## Design Identity

- **Theme:** Deep dark purple / near-black background (`#0D0B1E` to `#130D2E`)
- **Accent color:** Vivid purple / violet (`#7C3AED`, `#9D5CF6`, highlighted `cross-functional` text in lavender)
- **Typography:** Clean modern sans-serif; large bold headings with a typing cursor animation effect (`|`)
- **Visual motif:** Radial orbital rings around a central logo mark — communicates a "center of gravity" in the skill universe
- **3D / Illustrative elements:** Apple Memoji-style avatar holding a MacBook; 3D gradient orbs (light bulb, dropper) as section decorations
- **Gradient blobs:** Soft radial purple gradients used as ambient lighting behind key sections

---

## Site Sections (in scroll order)

1. **Navbar** — Logo mark left, three nav links (Home, About, Lab)
2. **Hero** — Avatar + tagline ("A Designer who Judges a book by its cover"), animated typing headline, current role callout (e.g. Facebook)
3. **About / Bio** — Short paragraph; role title with blinking cursor typewriter effect
4. **Work Experience** — 2×2 grid of project/client cards with 3D icon, title, short description, "Learn More" CTA
5. **Skills / Stack** — Centered CTA copy ("cross-functional team"), two rows of tech stack icon badges (Figma, React, C, Node, OpenAI, JS, CSS3, XD, Next.js, Gatsby, Illustrator, Express, MongoDB), orbital rings animation around logo
6. **Featured Projects** — Alternating left/right layout; project title, description, mockup screenshot, GitHub/external link icons
7. **Contact** — Minimal section with intro line, email address, social icons (Instagram, GitHub, Google)

---

## Out of Scope (for this implementation)

- Backend or CMS — all content is static/hardcoded or driven by a local data file
- Authentication or admin panel
- Blog or Lab section content (nav link present but page not in scope unless specified)
- Dark/light mode toggle
- i18n / localization
